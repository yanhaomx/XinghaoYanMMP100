Final: Click below to watch my Final project 


        There was an Andy Warhol's exhibition at Whitney Museum early this year. I went to watch and started being interested in his works. 
        His subject are simple, yet his idea is a genius. I present four of his paintings in my video to show the idea that art is not far from real life 
        and art is for all of the people. 
        I utilize colors and shapes and edit them together in a cohesive way with music and highly dynamic style. I wish I deliver the spirit of art, pop and 
        modern with the short video can motivate the audience. 
        Technically, I use Photoshop to create each picture as frame, and I use After Effects to create a little bit short moving pictures. The sound of typing 
        keyboard is from Freesound.org. At end, I use Premiere to edit them together to create this stop-animation.
